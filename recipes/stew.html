STEW

This is a picture of fresh tomatoes, needed for our stew.

Ingredients

  • Fresh Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Fresh Peppers
  • Seasoning Cubes
  • Salt
  • Curry Powder
  • Thyme
  • and of course, Water
  1. In your cooking pot, put your freshly blended mixture of 'Tomatoes,Onions and Peppers, and allow to heat between 5-8 minutes
  2. Add a tablespoon each of your seasoning cube, curry powder and thyme, oohhlala, aint that a nice aroma :)
  3. Add a pinch of salt, to make it taste much better than the aroma it gives off
  4. Add water so ous prepared stew won't be so thick
  5. ...Voila!!
